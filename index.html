Alec, Miles and Jimbo, 3 Boys, 1 car. UK to Mongolia

What is the Mongol Rally?

The mongol rally is a charity event where we will drive from England to Mongolia in to raise money for charity! The objective is to complete the rally, getting past borders and breakdowns to reach the finish line! We are only allowed a car that cost less than Â£500 and with and engine capacity of 1.0L or less and have to make it to the end on our own - with no luxary AA assistance.

Why are we doing the Rally?

Between us, we have lots of energy and ideas. The Mongol Rally was brought to our attention and after eyeballing it for a few months we decided that it would feasable - but by no means easy. The Rally would be a marathon more suited to our skills and experiences than one on foot, and would be a huge opportunity to raise money for charities in the process - after all its not something that people do every day. With some charities close to our hearts chosen, we have started to fundraise. Between us, we are determined to get to Mongolia in 1 piece and without any support during the rally.
